Get started

#1 Registration and set up

The first step is to register an account and then create a restaurant. Also filling in the necessary information such as: menu, opening hours, address, ...

#2 Implementation on your website (optional)

In the second step, you'll be pasting the code into your existing page. If you do not have your website, we will be happy to help you create it or create an order page for you on the domain restaurantname.piknik.cc.

#3 Done, welcome to the online world

You can now receive orders directly from your website. New orders will arrive in the administrative panel, from where you can confirm or reject them. You will process the confirmed order as standard, as you are used to, for example, when ordering by phone.
